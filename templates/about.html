About CitizenAI

CitizenAI is a civic engagement platform powered by advanced artificial intelligence. It bridges the gap between citizens and governments by enabling intuitive communication, feedback, and issue reporting — all through a friendly AI assistant.

🔍 In Simple Terms

  • Ask the AI Assistant questions about services, laws, or procedures.
  • Submit your feedback, and let AI detect the sentiment.
  • Report concerns like broken streetlights, water issues, etc.

🚀 Key Features

  • 💬 AI Chat Assistant powered by IBM Granite
  • 🧠 Sentiment Analysis for citizen feedback
  • 📢 Concern Reporting for local issues
  • 📊 Insightful Dashboard for real-time overview

💡 Why It's Useful

CitizenAI promotes transparent governance, quick issue resolution, and increased trust. It gives every citizen a voice, while helping authorities understand community needs better.
